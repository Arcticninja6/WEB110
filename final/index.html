Timeline

The Trip lasted about a week. The timeline looks like this

  • Day 1: Flying in
  • Day 2: Visiting the Karnak Temple and Luxor Temple
  • Day 3: Valley of the Kings and the Temple of Hatshepsut
  • Day 4: Horus Temple in Edfu and the Kom Ombo Temple
  • Day 5: Philae Temple in Aswan
  • Day 6: Flying to Cairo from Aswan
  • Days 7 & 8: Experiencing Cairo — Pyramids of Giza, the GEM, the Saqqara Pyramid, and a market

I hope you enjoy this look into a once in a lifetime experience!
